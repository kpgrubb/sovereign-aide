SOVEREIGN FALL OF WORMWOOD

WELCOME TO APSYNTION

A far-future society builder. A tactical card game for 2–4 players. Forge your empire. Collect ten Echoes. Survive.

WIN CONDITION First player to score 10 Echoes wins[p. 4] (12 Echoes recommended for 2-player games)

YOUR DECK

Each faction deck contains 32 unique cards and 20 Echoes — 52 cards total. Your deck defines your faction's identity and strategy.

[p. 4]

CITIZENS

Talents and Drones are your primary resources — not money. They work facilities, health your units, and power your abilities.

[p. 6]

SIX PHASES

Each turn flows through Growth → Upkeep → Forge → Battle → Move → Draw. Master the rhythm and you master the game.

[p. 10]

THE GOLDEN RULE

If a card ever contradicts something in this rulebook, the card text overrides anything written here.
